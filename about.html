Our Why

Hunger shouldn't be a technology problem. Yet, many community food organizations are still managing complex logistics and client engagement with limited tools.

We created Food-N-Force to change that.

Our Story

Food-N-Force was founded by a seasoned Salesforce consultant and Solution Architect with years of experience driving transformation across government and enterprise clients. After witnessing the impact of digital innovation in these sectors, our founder recognized an opportunity to bring similar tools to underserved nonprofit communities.

We're starting in the DC metro area—where we live and work—and expanding from there. Our mission is bigger. We're here to help any organization, anywhere, that wants to do more with less and serve people with dignity and efficiency.
