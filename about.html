Our Why

Hunger shouldn't be a technology problem. Yet, many community food organizations are still managing complex logistics and client engagement with limited tools.

We created Food-N-Force to change that.

Our Story

Food-N-Force was founded by a seasoned Salesforce consultant and solution architect with years of experience driving transformation across government and enterprise clients. After witnessing the impact of digital innovation in large-scale environments, we saw an opportunity to bring these same tools to the organizations feeding our communities.

We're starting in the DC metro area—where we live and serve—but our mission is bigger. We're here to help any organization, anywhere, that wants to do more with less and serve people with dignity and efficiency.
